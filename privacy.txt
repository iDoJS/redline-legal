# Redline Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 2/23/26

This Privacy Policy explains how Redline collects, uses, and stores information.

## 1. Information We Collect

Redline collects and stores only information necessary to operate the Service.

This includes:

- Discord user IDs
- Discord server IDs
- Report details submitted by users
- Evidence attachments provided during reports
- Moderation action records
- Configuration data set by server administrators

Redline does not collect:
- Passwords
- Email addresses
- Private messages
- Discord message history outside of submitted reports
- Data unrelated to bot interaction

## 2. How Information Is Collected

Information is collected only when:

- A user submits a report
- A moderator executes a moderation action
- A server administrator configures the bot
- A user interacts with the bot’s commands

Redline does not scrape Discord, monitor servers passively, or access data without direct interaction.

## 3. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used to:

- Store and review scam reports
- Track confirmed scam cases
- Enable moderation tools
- Provide cross-server scam history where applicable
- Improve functionality and stability of the Service

## 4. Data Sharing

Redline does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Report information may be visible to:
- Authorized staff reviewing reports
- Participating servers where relevant scam history is displayed
- The reporting user and the reviewing staff

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary to provide the Service, unless removal is required by law or policy.

Requests for data review or removal may be submitted through the official Redline server.

## 6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Redline operates within Discord and is subject to Discord’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us through the official Redline Discord server.
